EXCURSIONS TO THE ECO-FARM ▼

You have a great opportunity to visit our farm, where we will show you the full cycle processing of food waste and production of biohumus, as well as the cultivation of environmentally friendly vegetables, fruits and berries. (For children 6-16 years old)

What will you know after the excursion?

  1. You will learn how we collect food waste from the city's population.
  2. How to preparate of "food" for the worm.
  3. What is a "wermifarm" and why we need earthworms.
  4. What is biohumus and how is it collected, dried, and sifted.
  5. What does biohumus give to plants and how does it help them.
  6. What greenhouses are for and what they grow.
  7. What vegetables can be called environmentally friendly.
  8. Tasting of fresh vegetables "from the Bush".
  9. What fruits and berries grow in the garden.
  10. Who lives in the poultry yard. Familiarity with animals and birds.
  11. Picnic in the fresh air. PHOTOSESSION.
  12. Active games on the territory of the Eco-school.
